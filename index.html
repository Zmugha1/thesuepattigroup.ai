How do I find a good realtor in Lake Country?

Look for seven things: years of local experience, verified reviews from real clients, deep neighborhood knowledge, responsive communication, REALTOR membership (which includes a fiduciary code of ethics), a track record of closed sales near your price point, and a clear answer when you ask why you should hire them. We wrote a full guide that walks through each one. Read it here.

Why work with a team instead of a solo agent?

A team gives you two practical advantages. First, you get two experienced opinions on every important decision, especially listing strategy and negotiation. Second, you have someone available when one team member is on a showing or in a closing. The Sue Patti Group is 60+ years of combined experience working as one team for you.

What's the difference between a realtor and a real estate agent?

A real estate agent is licensed by the state. A REALTOR is an agent who is also a member of the National Association of REALTORS and is bound by its Code of Ethics, including a fiduciary duty to clients. Sue and Jason are both REALTORS.

Should I use Zillow or Realtor.com to find an agent?

Use those platforms to research listings and read reviews. They are not great for matching you with the right agent because their lead-routing systems often send you to whoever paid for that zip code, not whoever knows your market best. Choose your representation based on a conversation with a real human you trust.

How long does it take to sell a home in Lake Country Wisconsin?

Strongly-priced and well-prepped homes in the $400K-$650K range often go quickly when listed in spring. Higher-end homes ($800K+) typically take longer because the buyer pool is smaller. Country and lake-front properties are more seasonal. We give you a realistic timeline before listing your home, not a hopeful one.

What does it cost to use The Sue Patti Group?

In Wisconsin, real estate commissions are negotiable between you and your agent. We will walk you through how commissions work in your specific situation before you sign anything. There is no charge for the initial conversation or home valuation.
