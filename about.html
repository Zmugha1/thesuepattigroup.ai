Jason obtained his real estate license in 2005. Soon after, he was commissioned to manage a retail store in Waukesha, an experience that sharpened the skills that aid him in Real Estate. He became a great leader and a strong negotiator, with a tenacious work ethic and a mind for problem-solving. Within two years, he was responsible for opening a new, larger location.

In 2012, Jason made the decision to return to Real Estate. His roots in and passion for the Lake Country area drives his desire to connect buyers with new homes and sellers with compatible buyers. Jason moved to Delafield in 1994 and has since been devoted to the Lake Country area.

Lake Country has so much to offer: great community and plenty of recreation. Fishing, water-skiing, hiking, mountain biking are only some of the things Jason enjoys in the area. With its constant growth, excellent restaurants, and friendly atmosphere, there is no doubt Lake Country will continue to enchant him for years to come.
