Delafield Jason's Home

53018 | Waukesha County | Lake Country anchor
1994Jason since
$612KMedian price

Delafield is the upscale anchor of Lake Country. Sitting on the shores of Nagawicka Lake, this community offers historic downtown charm, premier dining, and some of the most desirable waterfront real estate in southeastern Wisconsin. Jason moved to Delafield in 1994 and has lived here ever since.

Why we know Delafield

When Jason says he is a Lake Country specialist, he means it literally. Three decades calling Delafield home. He fishes, water-skis, hikes, and bikes the same trails his clients are choosing this neighborhood for. He knows the lake levels, the tax assessment patterns, and which streets back up to what.

What makes Delafield special

Easy I-94 access puts Milwaukee just 25 minutes away. The Naga-Waukee State Trail runs through town, connecting hiking, biking, and cross-country skiing year-round. Summer brings the lake to life with boating, paddleboarding, and waterfront dining at landmarks like Andrew's and Lake Five.

Oconomowoc is one of the most active markets for The Sue Patti Group. Three lakes (Lac La Belle, Fowler, Oconomowoc Lake) give you historic mansions on the water, charming in-town homes, and the rapidly growing Pabst Farms development on the east side. There is a price point and lifestyle for nearly every buyer.

Recent Sue Patti Group sales here

1340 Orchard Circle ($530K, sold by Jason in 8/2024). 335 West 2nd Street ($367K, sold by Sue in 9/2025). Both happy clients, both smooth closings. We know the market here at every price point.

What makes Oconomowoc special

Historic Lac La Belle is lined with Gilded Age estates that still carry the names of the families who built them. Downtown Oconomowoc is having a moment with new restaurants, breweries, and shops. Pabst Farms anchors the east side with retail, medical, and new construction housing. Aurora Medical Center is the largest employer in Lake Country.

Ixonia Country Living

53036 | Jefferson County, west of Lake Country
$190KRockvale Rd land sale (recent)

Ixonia is what Lake Country was 30 years ago. Country roads, working farms, larger residential lots, fewer subdivisions. Just west of the Waukesha County line in Jefferson County, Ixonia gives buyers acreage and quiet at a price below most of Waukesha County while keeping access to Oconomowoc Area Schools.

Recent Sue Patti Group transaction in Ixonia: a Rockvale Road property closed at $190,000 in September 2024. Country properties require careful evaluation: well, septic, soil, outbuildings, and zoning all matter more than in subdivision homes.
